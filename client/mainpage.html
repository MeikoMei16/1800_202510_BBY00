TogetherNow

We aim to address the challenges seniors face, such as social isolation by bridging the gap between elders, youth, families, and communities. Seniors also have difficulty accessing essential resources. Our platform will foster meaningful connections by enabling youth and others to engage with elders, plan visits to learn about their cultural roots, and, where/when possible, provide care and support. We hope to strengthen intergenerational bonds and improve the well-being of our elders.
